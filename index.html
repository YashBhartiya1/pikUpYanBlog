About this place

Welcome to Dharamshala

Dharamshala (Devanagari: धर्मशाला; ITRANS: Dharmashala; IAST: Dharmaśālā) is a Hindi word (derived from Sanskrit) that is a compound of dharma (धर्म) and shālā (शाला). Literally, "House or place of Dharma".

Dharamshala is the winter capital of Himachal Pradesh, India, as well as the site of the Tibetan Government-in-exile. It serves as administrative headquarters of the Kangra district after being relocated from Kangra, a city located 18 km away from Dharamshala, in 1855. Dharamsala is located in the Kangra Valley, in the shadow of the Dhauladhar mountains.
