Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ's)

PikUPYan is a self-drive bike rental service that allows individuals to rent bikes for a short period, typically ranging from a few hours to several days. It operates on a membership-based model, where users can sign up for a membership and then book bikes through their website or mobile app.

With PikUPYan, users can choose from a variety of bike models and sizes based on their preferences and requirements. The service provides the flexibility and convenience of self-drive, allowing users to pick up the bike from designated locations and return it after their rental period is over.

With PikUPYan, you can experience the convenience of online booking. Rent a bike & start traveling with zero paperwork requirements! PikUPYan gives you the freedom of self-drive! With budget bike rental solutions, PikUPYan offers the best offers, the cheapest prices and a wide range of bikes to choose from. The booking process with PikUPYan is user-friendly and convenient. PikUPYan provides 24/7 on-road assistance to ensure customer safety and support in case of any issues during the rental period. This gives customers peace of mind and prompt assistance whenever needed.

PikUPYan offers various packages to cater to different needs and preferences of its users.

1. Hourly Packages: These packages are designed for short trips or uick errands. Users can rent a bike for a few hours, typically starting from a minimum of 1 hour, and pay for the duration they use the bike. Hourly packages are suitable for shorter distances or when you need a bike for a short period of time.

2. Daily Packages: Daily packages are ideal for longer trips or when you need a bike for the entire day. With this package, you can rent a bike for a full day, typically 24 hours, and enjoy the flexibility to travel as per your convenience. Daily packages are commonly used for weekend getaways, sightseeing, or business trips.

3. Weekly Packages: If you require a bike for an extended period, PikUPYan offers weekly packages where you can rent a bike for a week or more. These packages provide cost savings compared to daily rentals, making them suitable for longer vacations, work assignments, or when you need a bike for an extended duration.

It's important to note that the availability of specific packages and their pricing may vary based on your location and the bike models offered by PikUPYan. It is recommended to visit the official PikUPYan's website or contact their customer support for detailed information about the packages available in your area.
