Privacy Policy for PikUPYan

At PikUPYan, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by PikUPYan and how it is used.

Log Files

Like many other Web sites, PikUPYan makes use of log files. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
